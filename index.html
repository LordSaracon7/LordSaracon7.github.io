About Benjamin Valdez

Benjamin Valdez was born in Springfield, MO on July 24th, 2001 and was raised in Marshfield, MO. He grew up in a country neighborhood with his parents and two brothers. Shortly after his birth, his oldest brother Emilio, left for the naval academy and as a result, was seen less than his other brother, Alejandro. He spent most of his childhood living with Alejandro, and his parents. In 2012, his father sadly passed away and around the same time, his brother Alejandro was about to leave for Kansas City to attend college, so the household fell to two members, Ben and his mother. For a while, he lived life like this with his mother who was quite ill and growing worse over the years. Life was hard, but still enjoyble, and he soon graduated High School. Unfortunatly, months after his graduation, his mother finally succumb to her illness and Ben was left with the house to take care for. His brothers were living far away but he had family next door, so he made it by in college with help from them and his brothers too. Later on, his house was sold to a family member to take care of and Ben acquired a house for rent with his girlfriend, and lives there presently. Though his life has been quite tragic, he still cherishes the times from childhood and still experiences happiness in life. He is hardworking and makes it his mission to have a good life and to be happy.

Work Experience

Benjamin has worked at the fast food restaurant Taco Bell for nearly 3 years. During that time, he has worked days and nights multiple times a week while attending college at the same time. For a long time now, he has primarily become a closer for the store and is regarded as one of the best closers in fact. After being offered the position by his boss, Ben took up the manager position in recognition of the hard work he does for the store and how good of a closer he is. Being his first job and with being a closer, Ben has aquired much experience and has gained a strong work ethic. He knows, going forward, that he will be a very hard worker and do his best job possible.

Education

Ben received his eduation from the Marshfield R-1 School District and graduated Marshfield High School with a GPA of 3.8 and a magna cum laude, along with a handful of scholarships. He then moved on to attend Ozarks Technical Community College for his College Education and he plans to finish his associates this fall. He plans to finish his associates and see where he wants to go from there.
